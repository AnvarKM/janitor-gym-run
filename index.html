How to Play

  • Tap anywhere to jump.
  • Leap over lifters, treadmills, bikes, barbells and cable machines.
  • Run under the pull-up bars — don't jump into them!
  • The longer you survive, the faster it gets. Beat your high score.
  • Try not to break Samanta, your trusty mop.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Janitor Gym Run does not collect any data.

The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no network connection. It does not gather, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Your high score is saved only on your own device and is never sent anywhere. Deleting the app removes it.

Because no data is collected, none is shared with third parties.

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page.
